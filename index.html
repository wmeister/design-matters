ABOUT

Design Matters LLC is a full service architecture firm based in Salisbury, MD. Our practice is owned and operated by father-son team Daniel Moreno-Holt and Carlos Moreno.

Design Matters uses a network of expert freelancers to bring in required expertise on a project by project basis. Regardless of the size, type, and complexity of your project we can assemble the right team to bring your work into fruition.
